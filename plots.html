Welcome to Data Visualization of Different Datasets

This HTML page contains data visualization for Cars, Cerels, Film, Grocery Store Survey and Mutual Funds datasets. I have used Tableu to generate visualiztion for all the datasets. You can start scrolling down or click on each dataset names provided in the top to view the data visualization

Data Visualization for Cars

In this bar graph, I tried to showcase the Acceleration, Horsepower and MPG of different car manufacturers of American, European and Japanese origin. The graph also gives comparision of these three features among different manufacturers.

In general, we can see that most of the American origined manufacturers provides better Horsepower and Acceleration. Better MPG is provided by Japanese manufacturers. Also, We can see that Ford and Chevrolet have better values in terms of all three feautres in American region, Datsun and Toyato have better values in Japan Region and vw in European region.

Data Visualization for Cereals

The below graph gives the nutritional details of cereals produced by 7 different manufacturers.

We can see that almost all the manufacturers concentrated on providing more calories in their cereals. The average calories range between 86 to 115. Along with the calories they have concentrated on providing essential vitamins to the user. They made sure that their product does not contain or contains very less amount of fat and protiens.

Data Visualization for Films

The below timelined graph shows the comparision of award winning films vs un awarded films released over the period of 1920 to 1993 on different subjects. We can see the details of subjects in legends of the graph.

We can see from the graph that the number of films released in Drama, Comedy and Action had increased after 1980. The number of award winning movies were very less. Also from the graph it's evident that the films in Drama category got more awards. Comedy movies stand second in the number of awards recieved.

Data Visualization for Grocery Survey

The below packed bubble graph gives the comparision of grocery shopping done by males and females in different professions. The visualization also captures the mode of payment used for shopping

From the graph it's evident that females does more grocery shopping than the males. Also, we can see that cash is the major mode of payment followed by store card. The graph also gives the details of sum of purchase amount under each category.

Data Visualization for Mutual Funds

The below treemap gives the details of 6 different mutual funds. The treemap shows the net assest hold by each mutual fund. Also we can see the YTD value for each

From the graph it's clear that Large Value has the more number of net assets, however Large Growth has the better YTD ratio. Also, Small Value has the least YTD value.
